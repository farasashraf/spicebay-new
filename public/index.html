About us

SpiceBay PropTech Pvt Ltd

SpiceBay PropTech is a premier property management and real estate services firm based in the vibrant tech hub of Kakkanad, Kerala. We specialize in bridging the gap between property owners and seekers by providing a seamless, tech-enabled experience for managing and discovering quality living spaces.

Guided by our motto—"Homes · Spaces · Investments"—we are dedicated to being your trusted property partner.

Whether you are looking for a fully furnished apartment in Edappaly or a premium 3 BHK in the heart of Kakkanad, SpiceBay offers curated listings that prioritize comfort, modern aesthetics, and value.
